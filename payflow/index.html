How It Works

Five steps. Zero spreadsheets.

01

Create a project & define milestones

Break your client engagement into billable phases. "Phase 1: Wireframes — $3,000. Phase 2: Development — $7,000."

02

Assign subcontractors with rates

"Designer gets $1,200 for Phase 1. Developer gets $4,000 for Phase 2." PayFlow tracks who gets what.

03

Invoice your client per milestone

Generate a professional invoice with a Stripe payment link. Send it. Track when they open and view it.

04

Client pays → subs get paid automatically

When the payment hits, PayFlow splits it. Your designer's $1,200 goes to their account. Your $1,800 stays in yours. No manual transfers.

05

See your real profit

Not revenue. Profit. Per project, per milestone, per month. "Project Acme: $10K revenue, $6.5K sub costs, $3.5K net."
