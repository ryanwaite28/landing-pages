Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025 · Effective: January 1, 2025

Placeholder notice: This is a draft privacy policy for pre-launch purposes. A fully reviewed policy will be published before CivicFund accepts real user registrations or payment information.

CivicFund ("we", "us", or "our") operates civicfund.us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our platform.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile information: information you provide when creating an Organizer, Contributor, or Worker profile, including location data for project discovery.
  • Payment information: payment processing is handled by Stripe. We do not store raw card numbers or bank account details. Stripe's privacy policy applies to payment data.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, and interactions with the platform for analytics and improvement purposes.
  • Communications: messages you send through the platform, including project group chats and direct messages.
  • Waitlist submissions: email addresses submitted through our pre-launch waitlist form.

How we use your information

  • To operate the platform and facilitate project funding, hiring, and payments.
  • To send transactional emails (pledge confirmations, project updates, payout notifications).
  • To notify you of activity relevant to your projects or contributions.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including financial regulations.

Information sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in these circumstances:

  • Stripe: payment processing and Stripe Connect for worker payouts.
  • Amazon Web Services: infrastructure, file storage, and email delivery (SES).
  • Legal requirements: when required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger or acquisition, with notice to users.

Data retention

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. Financial transaction records are retained for a minimum of 7 years as required by applicable financial regulations. You may request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us, subject to legal retention requirements.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@civicfund.us.

Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and security. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

Children's privacy

CivicFund is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to this policy

We will notify registered users by email of material changes to this policy at least 30 days before they take effect.
